Protecting Life on Land

Introduction

Life on land focuses on protecting forests, biodiversity, and ecosystems. Human activities like deforestation and pollution threaten natural habitats.

Importance of Biodiversity

Biodiversity helps maintain ecological balance and supports life systems. Every species plays a role in sustaining the environment.
